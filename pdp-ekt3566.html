Intelli Kids Phonics is an interactive learning game set powered by a unique cordless pen. The pen lights up every time your child taps the correct answer on the sheet — that little glow is your child's first step on a creative learning journey.

Designed for kids aged 2 and above (with a little parent support), it strengthens the early-learning bond while helping kids remember alphabets, numbers, counting and word associations. Once they get the hang of it, they can play and self-check on their own.

Setup is quick: place the base plate down, drop in an activity sheet, and let your child tap away with the pen. Challenge them with new and twisted alphabet formats and watch their face light up with every right answer.

  • 1 cordless light-up pen
  • 10 double-sided activity sheets
  • 1 base plate
  • Quick setup guide
  • Place the base plate on a flat surface
  • Put one activity sheet on the plate
  • Tap the circle next to the answer with the pen
  • The pen glows on every correct answer
